📖 How to Use

▲
1

Set Durations

Set Focus and Break minutes. Default: 25 min focus + 5 min break.

2

Name Your Task

Type what you're working on — it gets saved with each session log.

3

Start & Minimise

Hit ▶ and switch tabs or minimise freely — the Web Worker keeps counting.

4

End Early

Click ⏹ End Now to stop mid-session. The time completed is saved and the remaining time shows in red in the log.

5

Break Mode

After focus, switch to Break and start. After the break, tap ⏭ or start focus again.

6

Quick Notes

Jot down ideas in the Notes panel on the right — they sync with your main Notes page.

🔔 Background timer: Uses a Web Worker — stays accurate even when tab is hidden. Allow notifications for end-of-session alerts.

💡 Laptop sleep: The timer pauses when your laptop sleeps — keep it awake during sessions via Power Settings.
